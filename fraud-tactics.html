FRAUD-TACTICS

Explore more to beware of scams !

Phone & WhatsApp

Scammers may contact you pretending to be a company or individual. Never give out personal information or click on suspicious links.

Fake Business Emails/Texts

Don't trust unsolicited emails or texts claiming to be from a company. Contact them directly using verified channels.

Impersonation

Legitimate companies will never ask for confidential information through calls, texts, or emails. If unsure: Hang up and report the incident.

Remote Access

Never grant remote access to your device to strangers claiming to fix issues.

Job Scams

Be wary of offers with unrealistic salaries or pressure tactics. Never send money to potential employers.

Cryptocurrency Scams

Don't rush into investments based on unsolicited offers. Verify information directly with the company.
